Typical tech interview questions for Google.

1. Explain a technical challenge you faced and how you solved it.

2. Design a scalable web service.

3. Walk me through a recent project you worked on.

4. How do you optimize a slow database query ?

5. Expain the difference between a process and a thread.

6. How would you handle a disagreement on a technical team ?

7. Describe a time you took a leadership role.

8. What is Big O notation, and can you give an example ?

9. How do you ensure you code is secure ?

10. Explain a situation where you had to debug a complex problem. Let me know which one you'd like to focus on first ?
